KEYBOARDING
OUR MOST VALUABLE TOOL

Keyboarding is something that comes along with computer science, keyboards(and mice) are our main way of interfacing with our machines. Everyone starts in different spots and a big way to make your assignments go by faster is to get comfortable with the keyboard you'll be spending 4 years with.
