Why You Should Study Computer Science

Computer science is more than just being able to program, it encompasses how computers work on the theoretical, software, and hardware levels. CS is all about the science of computers, an interest in how things work, and a desire for lifelong learning and more.

A large portion of your time studying computer science will probably be spent on coding projects. Because of this, if you enjoy solving problems and/or creating solutions to various challenges, then you might enjoy programming. The process of coding can be frustrating, but the exhilaration of finishing a project makes it all worth it.
