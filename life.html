Learn How To Be A Life Learner

In most jobs within this area of study, you will be encouraged to become a lifelong learner or someone who is willing to continue to learn since the field of computer science is continually growing.

There is a lot that can be gained from continually seeking out the newest security leak or AI breakthrough. Keeping up to date on current industry news and learning more in different areas keeps the mind working and allows for better work opportunities as well as opening you up to the many communities within the field of technology.

Never stop learning.

There will always be new infomation out there. Having an innate sense of curiosity and the willingness to seek out more can be highly beneficial in your work life as well as personal life. Picking up small hobbies somewhat related to your field can also potentially come in handy later. You'd be surprised the many things that come up at conventions or meet-ups. The most important thing really is understanding the value of knowledge. Ask professors, counselors, friends, and even well known people in the industry what is interesting to get into. Even if you aren't a programmer, learning a new language can be fun.
