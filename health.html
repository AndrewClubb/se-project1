The Importance of Mental Health

Take Breaks. Drink Water. Socialize.

A lot needs to be juggled while getting a degree, but not allowing all the bugs or blue screens get to you will be the most important thing to keep in mind. Your work is valuable and you can pour your heart and soul into it; but don't forget the need to take a break, drink some water, and socialize with friends and family. It doesn't hurt to take time to stretch and give your mind some time to recoop after working on the same thing for several hours.

Don't take everything so seriously.

Another key thing to maintaining good mental health is knowing that it's not always going to be perfect, grades are great to keep up but they aren't everything, and it is going to be hard. There will be many ups and downs but we all go through them. Pacing yourself and accepting the hurtles will make the journey an easier one to traverse. Ask your upper classmen for help and don't be afraid to reach out to your peers, as well as your professors; they are more than willing to help wherever needed. Work to the best of your ability and best of luck.
